**This addendum is a legally binding supplement to the MIT License above and takes precedence over any conflicting terms.**

### Purpose and Scope
The CHIMERA Framework is designed exclusively for authorized red team operations, security research, and defensive cybersecurity training. This framework simulates advanced persistent threat (APT) techniques to improve organizational security posture.

### Permitted Uses
The Software may only be used for the following purposes, subject to all applicable laws and regulations:

1. **Authorized Red Team Operations**: Penetration testing and security assessments conducted by certified professionals with explicit written authorization from the target organization.

2. **Academic and Research Purposes**: Security research, academic studies, and publications in peer-reviewed venues.

3. **Internal Security Training**: Employee awareness training and security education programs within organizations.

4. **Personal Development**: Individual learning and skill development in controlled, isolated environments.

### Strictly Prohibited Uses
The following uses are explicitly forbidden and constitute violations of this license:

1. **Unauthorized Access**: Any deployment without explicit written consent from all target participants and legal authorization from relevant authorities.

2. **Credential Theft or Harvesting**: Collection, storage, or transmission of actual user credentials, even temporarily.

3. **Harassment or Intimidation**: Targeting individuals for personal gain, revenge, or malicious purposes.

4. **Election Interference**: Any use that could influence democratic processes or public elections.

5. **Financial Fraud**: Business email compromise (BEC) or other fraudulent activities for monetary gain.

6. **Espionage**: Intelligence gathering for foreign governments or unauthorized entities.

7. **Weaponization**: Modification or distribution of the Software for offensive military or paramilitary operations.

8. **Mass Surveillance**: Large-scale monitoring without proper legal oversight and participant consent.

### Legal Certification Requirements

#### For Red Team Operations:
- **Written Authorization**: Must possess explicit written permission from target organization leadership.
- **Legal Review**: Authorization must be reviewed and approved by qualified legal counsel.
- **Insurance**: Operations must be covered by appropriate professional liability insurance.
- **Incident Response Plan**: Must have documented procedures for handling security incidents.

#### For Academic Research:
- **IRB Approval**: Research must be approved by an Institutional Review Board (IRB) or equivalent ethics committee.
- **Data Protection**: All participant data must be handled in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

#### For Commercial Training:
- **Compliance Documentation**: Must maintain records demonstrating compliance with all applicable laws.
- **Participant Consent**: All training participants must provide informed, voluntary consent.

### Technical Safeguards
Users of this Software must implement the following technical controls:

1. **Consent Verification**: All campaigns require real-time consent validation before execution.
2. **Kill Switch**: Immediate termination capability must be operational at all times.
3. **Geographic Restrictions**: Campaigns limited to pre-approved geographic regions.
4. **Rate Limiting**: Prevent mass-scale operations through configurable limits.
5. **Audit Logging**: Complete audit trail of all operations and participant interactions.
